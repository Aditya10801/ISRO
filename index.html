ISRO

THE INDIAN SPACE RESEARCH ORGANISATION.

Overview
ISRO, India's space agency under DOS, develops space technology for national needs. It conducts satellite missions, remote sensing, and promotes science education. Headquartered in Bengaluru, it has centers in Thiruvananthapuram, Bengaluru, Sriharikota, Valiamala, Ahmedabad, and Hyderabad. The Chairman oversees activities and leads the Space Commission, which formulates policies and oversees the Indian Space Programme's implementation.
